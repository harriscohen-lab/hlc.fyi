# Harris Cohen

Tech / copyright attorney and product leader with 10+ years of experience at YouTube and Google. Passionate about keeping platforms safe for creation and innovation.

## Experience

### Associate General Counsel — OpenAI  
`2024–present`

- Counseled product and research teams on development of agentic AI, search, and content generation features.
- Led development of tools for copyright owners regarding use of content for training, including engagement with rights owners, regulators, and civil society.
- Developed content moderation tools and systems for compliance with the EU Digital Services Act and UK Online Safety Act.

### Senior Counsel — Content, Google  
`2023–2024`

Formulated legal strategy on content regulation and platform liability issues, including:

- Generative AI features across Google and YouTube  
- Application of the EU Digital Services Act, UK Online Safety Act, and similar laws  

### Group Product Manager — User Safety, Google Search  
`2021–2023`

Led team keeping users safe, comfortable, and in control using Google Search (iOS and Android apps).

- Enabled reporting of privacy issues  
- Improved evaluation of information trustworthiness
